# Shadow Systems: Modern Cyber Security Paradigms

As the digital landscape crawls deeper into encrypted tunnels, our defensive strategies must evolve. Traditional firewalls are no longer sufficient against the sophisticated, decentralized threats emerging from the shadows.

## The Shift to Zero-Trust

In a world where origins are obscured, trust becomes the primary vulnerability. Zero-Trust Architecture (ZTA) assumes the network is already compromised, requiring rigorous verification for every single node interaction. 

## Neural Network Defense

We are beginning to see the rise of AI-driven security modules that can detect anomalous patterns in encrypted traffic without ever decrypting the data itself. This is the new frontline of digital defense.
